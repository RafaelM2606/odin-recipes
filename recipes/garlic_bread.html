Garlic bread

Description

This Easy Garlic Bread recipe goes great with soups, pasta dishes, and casseroles.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 loaf French Bread
  • 3 Tablespoons salted butter
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. First, preheat the oven and place the bread, cut side up, on a foil-lined baking sheet.
  2. Next, melt the butter in the microwave and stir in garlic powder and dried oregano.
  3. Then, brush the butter mixture over the bread and bake for 4-6 minutes.
  4. Finally, slice and serve!
